Similarly, MLflow experiment tracking lets you record details about your machine learning runs, such as:

- The code or model you used  
- The parameters (like learning rate or number of trees)  
- The data you trained on  
- The results or metrics (like accuracy or loss)  

All this information is saved so you can compare different runs, reproduce results, and share your work with others easily.
